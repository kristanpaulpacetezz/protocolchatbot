PROTOCOLS

PROTOCOLS

Based on the book of the school protocol it was written that the student must always wear their school uniform properly, wear your ID's when entering the school. Don't drink alcohol and Don't use cigarette inside the school premises. Do not vandalize. Always keep the school clean. But due to the pandemic, the school protocols became more strict. University guards do not allow students to enter UCU if you do not have an important appointment or transactions to the instructors. If you have any transaction in the university, you must follow the school protocols where you must bring School ID or any proof that you are currently enrolled in the university such as registration form or screenshot of your portal. Next is your own ballpen. Please bring also your alcohol and facemask. However, if you are a returning student or a graduated student in the university, just tell it to the school guards and they will able to make you enter the university. AND DON'T FORGET TO FOLLOW THE HEALTH PROTOCOLS OF THE UNIVERSITY.

HEALTH PPROTOCOLS

Just wear your facemask upon entering the university. Make sure that you have an alcohol with you and your own ballpen. Please avoid taking off your mask. Still, follow the 1.5 meter distance and make sure to always sanitize. Your health is also important to the UCU. If you are feeling the common symptoms of having a COVID-19 please don't force yourself to go to the university. Always stay safe and God bless!
